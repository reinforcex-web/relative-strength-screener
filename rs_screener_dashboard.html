How to Use This Screener

A quick reference for reading the dashboard and interpreting signals.

What This Screener Does

This tool ranks stocks by relative strength — how much a stock is outperforming (or underperforming) its benchmark index across multiple time horizons (1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y). It combines price momentum, volatility adjustment, and industry-relative performance into a single Master Score, then overlays earnings revisions, trend quality, and acceleration to classify every name into actionable signals.

Signal Badges — 3 Columns

The screener classifies every stock along three independent dimensions. A stock can carry one badge from each column simultaneously (e.g., NEW LEADER + CONFIRMED + SECTOR ROTATION).

Leader — RS lifecycle stage
NEW LEADERTop-decile Master Score and top-quartile 1M RS — strongest and freshest momentum.
EXISTING LEADERTop-decile Master Score but 1M RS not top-quartile — strong multi-horizon RS that's currently cooling or consolidating.
FADING LEADERTop-quartile Master Score but 1M RS below median — historically strong RS that's losing near-term momentum.
Confirm — do earnings agree with price?
CONFIRMEDTop-quartile Master Score and top-quartile EPS revisions — price leadership backed by improving fundamentals. Highest conviction.
UNCONFIRMEDTop-quartile Master Score but bottom-half EPS — price-led without earnings support. Higher risk.
REVISION LEADERTop-quartile EPS revisions but bottom-half Master Score — fundamentals improving faster than price. Potential early setup.
Signal — opportunities and risk alerts

This column shows one badge per stock. EXIT/WATCH take priority over early signals when both apply.

EXITThree warning signs triggered: below 50DMA, negative 1M RS, and negative acceleration. Consider reducing position.
WATCHTwo of three EXIT warning signs triggered. Monitor closely.
EMERGINGMid-rank stock with strong short-term RS, high acceleration, and price above 50DMA — building toward potential leadership.
ACCELERATINGMid-rank stock with top-quintile acceleration — RS is improving rapidly even if the composite hasn't caught up yet.
SECTOR ROTATIONStock in a sector where 1M RS just turned positive while 3M RS remains negative — classic early rotation signal.
Regime Bar

The regime bar at the top shows the benchmark index's return across all horizons and classifies the market environment (e.g., "Intermediate Downtrend," "Pullback in Strong Uptrend"). When the index trades below its 200-day moving average, this is flagged as "Below 200DMA." Use the regime to calibrate position sizing and risk appetite — strong uptrends favour adding to leaders; downtrends favour tighter stops and smaller positions.

Workflow Tips

Finding leaders: Filter by Leader → "NEW LEADER" or "EXISTING LEADER" and Confirm → "CONFIRMED" to see the highest-conviction names — strong RS backed by earnings.
Finding early setups: Filter by Early → "EMERGING" or "ACCELERATING" to catch names building momentum before they reach leader status.
Risk management: Filter by Exit → "EXIT" to see positions where the trend is breaking down across multiple signals.
Cross-market comparison: Switch between markets using the tabs to identify which regions have the deepest leadership pools and strongest regimes.
Tuning the engine: Use the Config tab to adjust composite weights, market cap thresholds, and signal parameters. Changes recalculate in real-time.
