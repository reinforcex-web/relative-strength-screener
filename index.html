Relative strength vs benchmark (stock return minus index return). Positive = outperforming.
Master Score
Composite percentile (0–100) blending volatility-adjusted and industry-relative RS across all horizons.
Acceleration
Whether RS is improving. High values flag names where momentum is freshly building.
Trend
Pass requires 2 of 3: price above 50DMA, golden cross, and price within 15% of 52-week high.

Leader Status

New Leader
Top-decile Master Score and top-quartile 1M RS. Strongest and freshest momentum.
Existing Leader
Top-decile Master Score, 1M RS not top-quartile. Strong multi-horizon RS, currently consolidating.
Fading Leader
Top-quartile Master Score, 1M RS below median. Historically strong RS losing near-term momentum.

Confirmation

Confirmed
Top-quartile Master Score and top-quartile EPS revisions. Price leadership backed by improving fundamentals.
Unconfirmed
Top-quartile Master Score, bottom-half EPS. Price-led without earnings support.
Revision Leader
Top-quartile EPS revisions, bottom-half Master Score. Fundamentals improving ahead of price.

Signals

Emerging
Mid-rank with strong 1M RS and high acceleration. Building toward potential leadership.
Accelerating
Mid-rank with top-quintile acceleration. RS improving rapidly.
Sector Rotation
Stock in a sector where 1M RS just turned positive while 3M remains negative. Classic early inflection signal.
Watch
2 of 3 risk signals triggered. Deterioration warning.
Exit
All 3 risk signals triggered. Strongest sell signal.
